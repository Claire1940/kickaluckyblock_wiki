<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner's Guide: How to Play">

If you are new to the experience, the core loop might seem chaotic at first. Your primary goal is to generate passive income by placing "Brainrots" on your plot. These units are earned by kicking the Lucky Block and successfully returning to your base. The farther you kick the block, the better the rewards, but the risk of the tsunami catching you increases significantly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Kick the Block">
    Approach the Lucky Block at your plot and interact with it. Your current Kick Power determines the initial distance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Reach the Zone">
    The block will land in a specific rarity zone (e.g., Rare, Epic, or Godly). A Brainrot unit will spawn at the landing site.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Tsunami Chase">
    As soon as you touch the Brainrot, a tsunami will begin to chase you. You must run back to your plot or a safe zone immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Bank and Place">
    Once you reach safety, the Brainrot is added to your inventory. Place it on your plot to start generating passive cash.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Upgrade and Reinvest">
    Use your earned cash to buy better weights and increase your run speed, allowing for deeper runs and better units.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Speed is Key">
Early in the game, do not ignore your **Run Speed**. If your Kick Power is too high but your speed is low, you will consistently lose your best Brainrots to the tsunami because you cannot make it back to safety in time.
</Callout>

</Section>
