### The Role of Luck and Server Boosts

Luck plays a significant role in the rarity of Brainrots you receive. While high kick power gets you to advanced zones, luck determines the quality of the drops within those zones.

| Luck Factor | Description | Impact on Brainrot Value | Cost (Robux) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Base Luck** | Standard luck, no boosts | Minimal | Free |
| **Server Luck (x2, x4, x8)** | Purchased boost for the entire server | Significant increase in rare drops | Varies (e.g., 1000 Robux for x8) |
| **Personal Luck Boosts** | Individual luck enhancers | Moderate increase | Varies |
| **Perfect Kicks** | Timing your kick meter perfectly | Slightly better drops within zone | Free (skill-based) |

<Callout type="success">
Investing in **Server Luck** (especially x8) is highly recommended when you have high kick power, as it dramatically increases your chances of getting Celestial or even Rainbow/Shadow Brainrots.
</Callout>

Even with high kick power, sometimes luck can be elusive. For instance, a player with 30 billion kick power might still get an OG Meow if server luck isn't optimized. Combining max kick power with max server luck is the optimal strategy for acquiring the most valuable Brainrots.
