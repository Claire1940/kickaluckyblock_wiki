## Optimizing Your Kick Power and Speed

A common pitfall is neglecting your movement speed. Even if you possess the strongest leg in the game, sending a block a million miles is meaningless if you cannot return to the start before the wave catches you.

### Essential Upgrade Path
1. **Balance:** Spend your initial earnings evenly between the Weight Shop and the Speed Stand.

3. **AFK Setup:** Before going offline, ensure your character is positioned to train automatically. While active play yields more, a consistent AFK setup generates significant passive income.

Rebirthing is the single most important factor for long-term growth. Because each rebirth resets your kick power, you should avoid doing so impulsively. Wait until you have maximized your current brain rot generation to ensure your post-rebirth progression is as smooth as possible.

## Maximizing Brain Rot Generation

Your "brain rots" are your primary source of passive income. To maximize your efficiency, focus on filling your available floor slots (up to 30) as quickly as possible.

### Efficiency Strategies
* **Leveling:** Never leave a brain rot at level 1. Once you have a steady income, dump your resources into upgrading them to level 75.
* **Mutations:** Once your base is full, replace common brain rots with mutated versions. Mutations provide a massive multiplier that far outweighs the base stats of standard units.
* **Organization:** Keep your highest-earning brain rots on the bottom floor. This makes it easier to track your primary income sources during quick check-ins.
