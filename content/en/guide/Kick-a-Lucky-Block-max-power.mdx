## Rebirth Timing for Faster Max Power Scaling

Rebirth is one of the strongest levers for **Kick a Lucky Block max power**, but only if timed well.

### When to rebirth
Rebirth when:
- Your cash multiplier jump is meaningful.
- You can recover lost kick power quickly with your current best weight.
- Your income units are already upgraded enough to rebuild fast.

Avoid rebirth when:
- You are one upgrade away from a major weight breakpoint.
- You still struggle to survive current wave zones.
- You are about to attempt a high-value kick with good timing.

| Rebirth Decision Check | Yes/No |
|---|---|
| Can I recover to previous power in a short session? | **Yes = Consider rebirth** |
| Will multiplier noticeably improve speed-buy potential? | **Yes = Rebirth is valuable** |
| Am I stuck because of speed, not power? | **Yes = Rebirth + income focus** |
| Am I in the middle of a major push attempt? | **Yes = Delay rebirth** |

In 2026 meta routing, rebirth is less about “reset now” and more about “reset when your next loop is faster than your current one.”
