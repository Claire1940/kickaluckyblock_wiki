<Section icon={Target} title="How to Activate Weather Events">

Activating the **kick a lucky block event** requires a specific sequence of actions. You cannot simply toggle the machine; you must have the necessary inventory items.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect Required Brain Rods">
    Farm the standard zones to obtain basic rods like Guest 666, Chickleta, or common Meows. You will need these as "fuel" for the machine.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to the Weather Machine">
    Located near the spawn area, replace the old virus station. Approach the toggle menu to see which weathers are currently unlockable.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Sacrifice and Summon">
    Select the desired weather (e.g., UFO for Alien blocks). The machine will consume your rods and trigger a 5-10 minute global event for the entire server.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Farm the Mutated Blocks">
    Run to the end of the stadium. During the event, lucky blocks will have a distinct visual change. Kick them to obtain the mutated variants.
  </Step>
</Steps>
