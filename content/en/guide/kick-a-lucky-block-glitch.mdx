<Section icon={Shield} title="Mastering Wave Immunity Glitches">
The tsunami is a constant threat in Kick a Lucky Block, capable of wiping out your hard-earned brainrots. Several methods have been discovered to potentially bypass this wave, offering a safe return to your base. While some methods might be patched, understanding the principles behind them can still be valuable.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Wi-Fi Disconnect Method">
    This glitch involves a precise timing of disconnecting your internet connection. After kicking a lucky block and as the wave approaches, quickly turn off your Wi-Fi. The game might register you as safe during the brief disconnection. Reconnect once the wave has passed. This method is highly timing-dependent and may have been patched.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The App Close Trick (Mobile)">
    For mobile players, kicking a lucky block and then immediately closing the Roblox app (without closing the tab) as the wave nears has been reported to make the wave disappear upon re-opening the app. This could allow you to return to your base safely.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Corner Clipping (Potentially Patched)">
    Some players have reported a glitch where slowly walking and jumping into specific corners of the map after a kick could allow them to clip into the environment, effectively bypassing the wave. This is often difficult to execute and may no longer be viable.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Practice Makes Perfect">
