## FAQ

**Q: How much power do I need for my first Kick a Lucky Block rebirth?**

A: You need exactly 1,000 (1K) kick power to perform your first rebirth. Subsequent rebirths will require significantly more power, such as 10K for the second tier.

**Q: Do I lose my money when I rebirth?**

A: Typically, rebirths reset your current kick power and sometimes your coins, but you keep your permanent upgrades, pets, and the multipliers gained from the rebirth itself. Always check the in-game rebirth menu for specific reset details.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get power for a rebirth?**

A: The most efficient method is to join the game's group for free boosts, buy the strongest weight available in the Weight Shop, and use a Power Potion while training.

**Q: Why should I use repairs in Kick a Lucky Block?**

A: Repairs help maintain the efficiency of your upgrades. As you buy more upgrades, your power build-up speed increases, and keeping everything repaired ensures you are training at the maximum possible rate.
