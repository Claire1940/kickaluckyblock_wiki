### Progression Steps for Optimal Rarity Drops

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Kick Power Boost">
    Start by acquiring basic weights like the **Wooden Stick** and **Bone Barbell**. These early upgrades significantly increase your Kick Power, allowing you to reach Rare and Epic zones more consistently.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Balance Speed and Power">
    As you approach Legend and Mythic zones, invest in Run Speed upgrades. Kicking farther means a longer journey back to your plot, and a fast return is essential to avoid the tsunami. A good balance ensures you don't lose valuable Brainrots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Strategic Weight Upgrades">
    Target major Kick Power jumps such as the **Copper Plate** and **Iron Plate**. These weights unlock access to Godly and Secret zones, where Brainrot earnings become substantial. Avoid overspending on minor upgrades that offer little impact.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Embrace Rebirths">
    Rebirths are critical for long-term progression. They reset your Kick Power but provide permanent cash multipliers. Time your rebirths after filling your plot with high-rarity Brainrots to maximize the multiplier's impact on your passive income.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Hunting for Mutations">
    Once you can consistently reach Divine, Hacked, and OG zones, focus on acquiring **Mutated Brainrots**. Mutations like Radioactive or Rainbow dramatically increase the value of an already rare Brainrot, leading to exponential cash gains.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Smart Farming Strategy">
Always aim to fill your plot with the highest rarity Brainrots you can safely retrieve. Regularly replace lower-value Brainrots with stronger ones as your Kick Power and zone access improve.
</Callout>

</Section>
