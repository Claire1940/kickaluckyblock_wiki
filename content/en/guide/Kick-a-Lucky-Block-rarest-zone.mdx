## Rebirth strategy: when to reset and when to hold

Rebirths can accelerate your route to the **Kick a Lucky Block rarest zone**, but mistimed resets stall progression. In 2026, the most efficient approach is “milestone rebirthing,” not constant rebirthing.

### Rebirth timing rules

| Situation | Rebirth? | Reason |

> **💡 Tip:** Treat rebirth as an economy tool, not a panic button. If your next weight is close, finish that purchase before resetting.
