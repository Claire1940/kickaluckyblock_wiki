In the addictive Roblox game, *Kick a Lucky Block*, players kick Lucky Blocks into various rarity zones to acquire "Brainrots" – unique items that generate passive income. The journey from the initial **Common** zone to the elusive **Celestial** zone requires careful planning, strategic upgrades, and precise kicking. Your kick distance directly determines which zone your Lucky Block lands in, impacting the rarity and value of the Brainrot you receive.

There are currently 11 distinct rarity zones, each presenting increasing challenges and rewards. As you progress through these **Kick a Lucky Block zones**, the requirements for kick power and the speed of the accompanying tsunami intensify, demanding continuous improvement of your character's stats.

### The Core Gameplay Loop

The gameplay in *Kick a Lucky Block* is centered around a concise yet layered loop:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Kick the Lucky Block">
    From the yellow zone, time your kick meter to achieve a Perfect Kick. This ensures maximum power and a luck boost for potential mutated Brainrots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Outrun the Tsunami">
    Immediately after kicking, a tsunami spawns. The further your block travels, the faster the wave. You transform into the Brainrot you just earned and must sprint back to your safe zone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Place Brainrot and Collect Income">
    Upon returning, place your new Brainrot on a base pedestal to start generating passive income. Collect this income by stepping on the green pad at your base.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Train Kick Power">
    Between kicks, visit the Weight Shop to lift weights and increase your kick power, preparing for higher-tier zones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Upgrade Speed">
    Buy Speed upgrades from the Run Upgrade Shop (left side of the safe zone) to handle the increasingly fast tsunamis in advanced zones.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Brainrot Management

Your Brainrots are your primary source of passive income. Managing them effectively is key.

| Action | Benefit | When to Apply |
| :----- | :------ | :------------ |
| **Place every Brainrot** | Consistent income, even low-tier ones | Immediately after acquiring, fill all pedestals |
| **Chase mutated Brainrots** | Up to 8x income multipliers | Once you reach Legendary/Mythic zones |
| **Skip Common/Rare upgrades** | Save coins for higher-tier Brainrots | Focus upgrades on Mythic rarity and above |
| **Sell low-value Brainrots** | Free up slots, small income boost | When transitioning to higher zones (e.g., from Legendary to Mythic) |

<Callout type="tip">
Brainrots generate cash even while you are offline. Make sure to check the daily earnings display at your base to monitor their contribution. Leaving your character AFK lifting weights while Brainrots generate income is a highly effective strategy.
</Callout>
