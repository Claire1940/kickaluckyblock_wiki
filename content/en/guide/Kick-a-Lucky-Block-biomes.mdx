  <Step num="4" title="Plot Management and Reinvestment">

  </Step>

<Section icon={Footprints} title="Run Speed and Tsunami Survival Guide">

As you progress through the **Kick a Lucky Block biomes**, the physical distance between the landing zone and your plot increases. Without sufficient Run Speed, you will be unable to outrun the tsunami, making your high Kick Power useless.

| Speed Level | Cost Range | Max Safe Zone | Survival Tip |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Level 1-10** | $0 - $50K | Common to Epic | Run in a straight line; don't jump. |
| **Level 11-25** | $50K - $1M | Legend to Mythic | Start upgrading speed every 5 power levels. |
| **Level 26-50** | $1M - $100M | Godly to Secret | Use the "Perfect Kick" to get a head start. |
| **Level 51+** | $100M+ | Divine to Celestial | Essential for endgame farming efficiency. |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Assess the Distance">
    Before kicking, check your current Speed level. If you just bought a new weight, your power might outpace your speed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Turn-Around">
    The moment the Brainrot appears, your character model changes. Immediately hold the 'W' key (or forward on mobile) to head back. Even a half-second delay can result in a loss in higher biomes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Banking the Reward">
    The Brainrot is only safe once you touch your plot's floor. Once banked, you can take your time placing it or upgrading your stats.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Speed Caps">
There is a diminishing return on speed upgrades at very high levels. Focus on reaching the "Safe" threshold for your current biome rather than over-spending on speed when you could be buying a better weight.
</Callout>

</Section>
