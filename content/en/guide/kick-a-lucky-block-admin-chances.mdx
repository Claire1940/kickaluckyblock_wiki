### Maximizing Kick Power and Run Speed

Your **Kick Power** determines how far you can launch a lucky block, directly impacting the rarity of the brainrot you can obtain. The further the kick, the more advanced the zone, and the rarer the brainrot. **Run Speed** is equally critical; after kicking a block, a tsunami spawns, chasing you back to your base. If caught, you lose the brainrot.

| Upgrade Type | Benefit                                     | How to Upgrade               | Important Notes                                   |
| :----------- | :------------------------------------------ | :--------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------ |
| **Kick Power** | Reach rarer brainrot zones                  | Lift weights at Weight Shop  | Higher power needed for Mythic (240K+) and OG (60M+) zones |
| **Run Speed**  | Escape tsunamis, secure brainrots           | Purchase at Run Upgrade Shop | **Upgrades are NOT reset on rebirth.** Mythic zones require millions in speed. |

Here are the reference kick power values needed to reach specific zones:

| Zone Rarity | Minimum Kick Power |
| :---------- | :----------------- |
| **Legendary** | **40,000+**        |
| **Mythic**    | **240,000+**       |
| **OG**        | **60,000,000+**    |
