Rebirth when all three are true:

- You can recover your previous kick power quickly.

### Speed-to-Distance Checkpoint Method

After each major power jump:

1. Do one excellent kick.
2. Test return margin (how close wave gets).
3. If margin is too small, buy speed before the next max push.
4. Only force perfect kicks once return margin is healthy.

This method reduces failed runs and improves your average resources per minute.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way to reach the Kick a Lucky Block strongest kick in 2026?**

A: Build power early, then maintain speed parity so long kicks remain collectible. Upgrade your top income source repeatedly, rebirth at efficient recovery points, and only push perfect kicks when your return margin is safe.

**Q: Should I focus on kick power or speed first?**

A: In early game, power gets you into better zones faster, but speed should be upgraded regularly. If returns start failing, speed becomes the immediate priority until survival is stable again.

**Q: Is rebirth worth doing as soon as it unlocks?**

A: Not always. Rebirth is best when multiplier gain clearly improves your next cycle and your kick power recovery is quick. If recovery is slow, farm a bit longer before resetting.

**Q: Why do my strongest kicks feel inconsistent?**

A: Usually because timing quality, return speed, and wave pressure are out of balance. Use excellent kicks for consistency, then shift to perfect launches only when your speed and economy can support the extra risk.
