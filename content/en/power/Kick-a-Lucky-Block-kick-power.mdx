## Mid-Game Strategy: Rebirth Timing, Speed Walls, and Income Spikes

By mid-game, the main issue becomes efficiency drift: you *can* progress with random upgrades, but your time-per-rebirth gets worse.

### Rebirth timing model

Use this simple rule:

- Rebirth when your next major training tier or speed jump takes too long relative to reset gains.
- Delay rebirth if your current setup can unlock a major income step quickly.

| Scenario | Rebirth Now? | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| You can rebirth in 1–3 short cycles | **Yes** | Multiplier gains are immediate |
| Next speed upgrade is unaffordable and wave deaths increase | **Usually Yes** | Reset improves earning slope |
| You are one purchase from a major income tier | **No (short delay)** | Take the income spike first |
| Kick power is high but zone rewards are inconsistent | **Yes** | Rebuild cleaner with better scaling |

In this phase, **Kick a Lucky Block kick power** still matters, but speed has equal weight. Many runs fail because players push into higher zones they cannot safely return from.

> **Warning:** If you lose high-value returns repeatedly, your effective progress per minute drops hard. Safer consistent loops often outperform risky max-range kicks.
