1. **Over-investing in one dimension**  
   Power-only builds hit return walls. Speed-only builds lose distance value.

2. **Ignoring kick quality timing**  
   “Perfect” attempts matter because they change lane depth and drop opportunities.

3. **Forcing Celestial too early**  
   If you’re touching Celestial but extracting OG-tier returns, farm OG/Hacked safely until speed rises.

4. **Leveling too many units at once**  
   Concentrate resources into top ROI units for better currency growth.

5. **No cycle structure**  
   Random play is slower than a fixed loop (AFK squat → speed buy → safe farm → push test).

| Mistake | Symptom | Fast Fix |
|---|---|---|
| **Power-only spending** | Reach far, return fails | Buy speed in the next 1–2 purchases |
