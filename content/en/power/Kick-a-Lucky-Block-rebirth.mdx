<Section icon={Trophy} title="Understanding Rebirth in Kick a Lucky Block">
Rebirth is the prestige system in Roblox's *Kick a Lucky Block*, allowing players to reset their Kick Power progression in exchange for permanent cash multipliers. This mechanic is vital for accelerating your earnings and reaching the advanced stages of the game. The core idea is to make each subsequent run faster and more profitable than the last.

The first rebirth becomes available once you reach 1,000 Kick Power and provides a permanent 2x cash multiplier. Subsequent rebirths will continue to stack these multipliers, making your Brainrots generate money at an increasingly rapid pace. This system encourages strategic gameplay, pushing you to optimize your plot and upgrades before committing to a reset.

<Callout type="tip">
Rebirth is most effective when you have a plot full of high-earning Brainrots, as the multiplier applies to your existing income, significantly boosting your passive cash flow.
</Callout>

### First Rebirth Requirements

| Requirement | Value | Effect on Game |
| :---------- | :---- | :------------- |
| **Kick Power** | 1,000 | Unlocks the first Rebirth option |
| **Cash Multiplier** | 2x | Permanent boost to all future cash earnings |
| **Reset Content** | Kick Power | Returns to base; weights must be re-purchased |
| **Kept Content** | Brainrots, Run Speed, Plot Slots | Essential for faster post-rebirth progression |

### Step-by-Step Rebirth Optimization

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Fill Your Plot with High-Value Brainrots">
    Before initiating a rebirth, ensure your plot is completely filled with the highest-earning Brainrots you can acquire. These will generate income at an accelerated rate immediately after your rebirth, thanks to the new multiplier. Focus on Brainrots from Mythic, Godly, or even Secret zones if you can safely retrieve them.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Upgrade Run Speed">
    As your Kick Power increases, you'll naturally kick Lucky Blocks farther. After rebirth, you'll need to re-train Kick Power. Having high Run Speed is critical to outrun the tsunami and safely return valuable Brainrots from distant zones. Prioritize Run Speed upgrades before and between rebirths.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Save Cash for Initial Weights">
    Although your weights reset, your cash does not. Accumulate a significant amount of cash before rebirthing. This allows you to quickly re-purchase essential early and mid-game weights like the Bone Barbell or Copper Plate, rapidly increasing your Kick Power again.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Time Your Rebirth">
    For early rebirths, hitting the 1,000 Kick Power requirement and rebirthing can be beneficial. However, for later rebirths, it's often more effective to push one or two zones deeper, collect even better Brainrots, and then rebirth. This ensures the multiplier applies to an even stronger income base.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Re-evaluate Weights and Zones Post-Rebirth">
    After rebirth, your increased cash multiplier will make previous weights more affordable. Use this advantage to quickly climb back to your preferred farming zones, or even push into new, higher-value zones faster than before. Each rebirth cycle should see you reaching higher income plateaus sooner.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Rebirth Skip and Common Mistakes">
While the Rebirth system is designed to aid progression, there are options like the Rebirth Skip and common pitfalls to avoid that can hinder your growth.

### The 99 Robux Rebirth Skip

The Rebirth Skip is a Game Pass available for 99 Robux. It allows you to perform a rebirth without meeting the Kick Power requirement. This can be a useful time-saver, especially if you're looking to quickly gain a cash multiplier without the grind. However, it's important to remember that this skip only grants the multiplier; you still need to train your Kick Power and acquire weights to reach far zones and get good Brainrots. It's a convenience, not a replacement for the core gameplay loop.

<Callout type="info">
The Rebirth Skip is primarily for convenience. While it grants the multiplier instantly, you still need to actively play and upgrade to make that multiplier truly effective.
</Callout>

### Common Rebirth Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Consequence | Mitigation Strategy |
| :------ | :---------- | :------------------ |
| **Weak Plot Rebirth** | Multiplier applies to low income, minimal benefit. | Fill plot with the best possible Brainrots before rebirthing. |
| **Ignoring Run Speed** | Lose valuable Brainrots to tsunamis after kicking farther. | Always upgrade Run Speed in tandem with Kick Power. |
| **Stockpiling Cash Too Long** | Delaying upgrades means slower income growth. | Invest cash into weights and Speed upgrades, then rebirth to multiply earnings. |
| **Rebirthing Too Early** | Gain a multiplier but can't quickly reach good zones again. | Push for a slightly better zone or Brainrot before the reset. |
| **Not Understanding Resets** | Surprise when Kick Power and weights are gone. | Review the "What Resets" table and plan accordingly. |
