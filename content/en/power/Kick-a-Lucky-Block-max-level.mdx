### Best Mutation Strategies
<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Target High-Rarity Zones">
    Mutations on already rare Brainrots (Mythic, Godly, Secret, etc.) yield far greater returns than on common ones. Focus your kicks on zones where the base Brainrot value is high.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Luck Events">
    Time your farming sessions around Global Luck events. These events significantly increase your chances of rolling mutated Brainrots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master Perfect Kicks">
    Perfect Kicks can sometimes offer luck bonuses or increased distance, improving your chances of both higher-zone Brainrots and mutations.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Consider Mutation Luck Gamepass">
    If available, the Mutation Luck gamepass can provide a consistent boost to your mutation rates, accelerating your endgame income.
  </Step>
</Steps>
