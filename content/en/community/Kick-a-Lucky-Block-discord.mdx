<Section icon={Shield} title="Tsunami Survival and Rebirth Guide">

Survival is just as important as kick power. The "Tsunami Survival" loop is what separates successful tycoons from those who stay stuck in the early game. Additionally, the Rebirth system allows you to reset your power for permanent multipliers.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Kick">
    Kick the Lucky Block from your plot. Ensure you are using your best equipped weight for maximum distance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Chase">
    As soon as the Brainrot appears, turn around immediately. The tsunami starts moving the moment the block lands.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Banking the Reward">
    Return to your plot or the safe zone. Place the Brainrot in an empty slot to begin generating cash.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Rebirth Decision">
    Upon reaching **1,000 Kick Power**, you can Rebirth. This resets your power but grants a **2x Cash Multiplier**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
