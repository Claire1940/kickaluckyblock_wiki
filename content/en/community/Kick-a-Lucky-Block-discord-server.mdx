To contribute meaningfully to the **Kick a Lucky Block discord server** discussions, you need to understand the fundamental mechanics. The game is a "Brainrot Tycoon," where your success is measured by your Kick Power and the rarity of the units on your plot.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Initial Kick">
    Stand at your plot and interact with the Lucky Block. Your current weight determines your Kick Power, which dictates how far the block travels.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Zone Landing">
    The block will land in a specific zone (e.g., Rare, Epic, or Mythic). The farther it goes, the better the Brainrot reward pool becomes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Tsunami Survival">
    Immediately after the kick, a tsunami will chase you. You must run back to your plot or a safe zone to "claim" the Brainrot. If caught, you lose the reward.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Plot Management">
    Place your Brainrots on your plot to generate passive income. Use this cash to buy better weights and upgrade your Run Speed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Strategy">
Don't just focus on Kick Power. In the **Kick a Lucky Block discord server**, the most common advice for beginners is to balance "Run Speed" upgrades. If you kick a block to the "Celestial" zone but can't run back fast enough, that high-value Brainrot is wasted.
</Callout>

One of the most discussed topics in the **Kick a Lucky Block discord server** is the Mutation system. Mutations are permanent multipliers applied to a Brainrot when it is rolled. These can turn a mediocre unit into a cash-generating powerhouse.

| Mutation Type | Multiplier | Chance | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gold** | 1.5x | 1 in 10 | Early game filler for empty slots |

| **Plasma** | 4.0x | 1 in 33 | Keep these on Mythic units |
| **Radioactive**| 8.0x | 1 in 333 | High-tier target for late-game players |
| **Rainbow** | 30.0x | 1 in 20,000 | The ultimate endgame goal |

<Callout type="info" title="Mutation Tip">
Mutations scale with the base value of the Brainrot. A "Rainbow" mutation on a starter "Common" Brainrot is significantly weaker than a "Gold" mutation on a "Godly" Brainrot. Always prioritize high-zone units first.
</Callout>

When you reach the endgame, your plot will have limited slots. The Discord community recommends replacing any non-mutated units with "Radioactive" or "Shadow" versions as soon as your income allows for consistent high-zone farming.
