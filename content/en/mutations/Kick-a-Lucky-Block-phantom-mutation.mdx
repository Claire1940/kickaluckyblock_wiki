Mutations are special enhancements that can randomly apply to **Brainrots** when you kick a Lucky Block. These enhancements provide multipliers to the Brainrot's base value, increasing both its cash-per-second income and its sale value. ### Step-by-Step Approach to Rolling Rare Mutations

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Achieve High Kick Power">
    Your primary goal is to consistently reach the highest rarity zones (Hacked, OG, Celestial). These zones offer the best base **Brainrots**, which are the most valuable candidates for any mutation, especially the Phantom. Invest in powerful weights like the **Mega Golden Barbell** or **Giant Gold Star Barbell** to maximize your kicking distance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master Perfect Kicks">
    Perfect Kicks can provide a significant boost to your luck, increasing the probability of rolling a mutation. Practice your timing to consistently land Perfect Kicks, turning a random mechanic into a more controlled advantage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize Global Luck Events">
    Keep an eye on in-game announcements for **Global Luck** events (e.g., 2x Luck, 4x Luck). These events dramatically increase mutation chances for all players. Combine these with your personal luck boosts for the highest possible odds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Invest in Mutation Luck Gamepasses">
    If available, purchasing a **Mutation Luck** gamepass is a direct way to increase your personal mutation odds. While it requires Robux, for serious players hunting the Phantom Mutation, this can be a worthwhile investment.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Prioritize Run Speed">
    Rolling a Phantom Mutation on a Celestial Brainrot is useless if you lose it to the tsunami. Ensure your **Run Speed** is sufficiently upgraded to safely return with high-value Brainrots from the farthest zones.
  </Step>
</Steps>
