## Common Mistakes That Slow Rainbow Celestial Progression

Even strong accounts lose tempo from a few repeated errors:

### 1) Rebirthing at the wrong time
Rebirth helps scaling, but not every moment is optimal. If you are one push away from a major zone threshold, hold rebirth and finish that milestone first.

### 2) Ignoring speed upgrades
Players chase kick power and then get caught by stronger waves. A failed run wastes more time than one extra speed purchase.

High power with poor timing underperforms medium power with consistent perfect kicks.

### 4) Trading for hype instead of utility
A flashy unit that doesn’t improve your farm loop can delay progress. Prioritize earnings, survivability, and consistency first.

### 5) Splitting focus too early across main and alt
Dual-account training can help, but only if both accounts have clear roles (farm account vs progression account). Otherwise, both end up mediocre.
