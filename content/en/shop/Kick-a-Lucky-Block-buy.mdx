## Smart stat balancing: power vs speed vs income

Players often assume “more power = faster progression.” In practice, **retrieval success rate** is the real KPI. If your power sends drops too far, but speed can’t support the distance, your expected value drops.

Use this decision matrix for every 15–20 minutes of play:

| Situation | Best Move | Why |
|---|---|---|
| You reach new rarity lane but fail returns | **Buy speed first** | Secures current gains before pushing farther |
| You return easily but drops are low value | **Buy power** | Opens higher reward bands |
| You return reliably and income feels slow | **Upgrade top earners** | Improves passive economy |
| You hit expensive stat walls | **Rebirth prep** | Resets for stronger growth curve |
| You’re tempted by random premium packs | **Delay and test baseline** | Prevents low-ROI spending |
