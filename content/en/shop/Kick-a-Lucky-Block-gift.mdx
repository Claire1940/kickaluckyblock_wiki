## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way to progress in Kick a Lucky Block gift?**

A: Balance power, speed, and passive income instead of maxing one stat. Most players gain more by securing consistent returns from mid/high zones than by forcing risky max-distance kicks.

**Q: Should I rebirth as soon as it unlocks?**

A: Not necessarily. Rebirth is strongest when your rebuild is quick and your passive income foundation is already efficient. If resets feel slow, scale current upgrades first.

**Q: Are luck boosts worth it in Kick a Lucky Block gift?**

A: Yes, but mainly after your baseline economy is stable. Luck boosts have better value when you can repeatedly reach deeper zones during the active window.

**Q: What causes late-game slowdown the most?**

A: Under-investing in movement speed. Many players have enough kick power to reach top bands but fail to recover rewards due to wave pressure. Speed tuning is a core late-game stat, not an optional one.
