Before spending, understand the loop:

4. Upgrade weights, speed, and base capacity  
5. Rebirth at value checkpoints  

Most waste in spending Robux in Kick a Lucky Block comes from ignoring step 2 (run-back reliability). If you can’t return fast enough, high-tier pulls lose practical value.
