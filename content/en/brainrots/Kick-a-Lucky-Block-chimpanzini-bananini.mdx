### Step-by-Step Farming Process

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Boost Kick Power">
    Invest in the best weights available to you. Higher Kick Power allows your Lucky Block to land in farther, rarer zones. Focus on significant power jumps like the Copper Plate or Iron Plate to quickly move past early-game zones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Upgrade Run Speed">
    As your Kick Power increases, the distance to retrieve Brainrots grows. Upgrade your Run Speed to ensure you can outrun the tsunami and safely return to your plot with your valuable finds. Losing a rare Brainrot to the tsunami is a significant setback.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Target High Rarity Zones">
    Once your Kick Power and Run Speed are adequate, aim for zones like Mythic, Godly, Secret, or Divine. These zones have a higher probability of dropping rare and ultra-rare Brainrots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Luck Events and Game Passes">
    Keep an eye out for Global Luck events or consider purchasing Game Passes like "Brainrot Luck" or "Mutation Luck." These can significantly increase your chances of rolling a rare or mutated Brainrot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Perfect Kicks">
    Mastering the timing of "Perfect Kicks" can also influence the landing distance and potentially the quality of your Brainrot rolls. Practice to consistently hit perfect timing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Plot Management and Rebirth">
    Ensure your plot has enough slots for new Brainrots. Replace lower-value Brainrots with higher-earning ones as you acquire them. Strategically use Rebirth to gain permanent cash multipliers, accelerating your ability to afford better upgrades and farm more efficiently.
  </Step>
</Steps>
