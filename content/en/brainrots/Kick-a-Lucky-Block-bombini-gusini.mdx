<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimizing Your Brainrot Collection and Plot Management">
Effective Brainrot management is key to sustained growth in **Kick a Lucky Block**. This involves not just acquiring high-value Brainrots, but also strategically placing them on your plot, upgrading them, and knowing when to replace them.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prioritize High-Rarity Brainrots">
    Focus your efforts on reaching zones that yield Epic, Legend, and Mythic Brainrots as soon as possible. These offer a much better return on investment for your time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Fill Your Plot Slots">
    Initially, fill every available plot slot with any Brainrot you acquire. Even low-value Brainrots contribute to passive income, which can fund weight upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Replace Low-Value Brainrots">
    As you acquire better Brainrots, replace your oldest or lowest-earning ones. Continuously upgrading your plot's inhabitants ensures your income keeps growing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade Mutated Brainrots">
    If you get a mutated Brainrot, especially a rare one, prioritize upgrading it. The multiplier effect makes these significantly more profitable than unmutated versions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Balance Kick Power and Run Speed">
    To safely bring back valuable Brainrots from distant zones, ensure your Run Speed is adequate. Losing a high-rarity Brainrot to the tsunami negates all effort.
  </Step>
</Steps>
