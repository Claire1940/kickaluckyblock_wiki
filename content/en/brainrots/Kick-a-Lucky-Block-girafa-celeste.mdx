The risk of losing a valuable Brainrot to the tsunami increases dramatically when you're kicking to distant zones like Girafa Celeste. <Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Kick and Observe">
    Kick the Lucky Block from your plot. Immediately note the landing distance and the rarity zone achieved. The farther it lands, the more critical your immediate response.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Instant Return">
    As soon as the Brainrot appears, transform into it and immediately head back towards your plot. Every second counts, as the tsunami begins its chase after your kick.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prioritize Speed">
    Run directly to safety. Avoid any detours or unnecessary movements. If the tsunami catches you before you reach your plot, the Brainrot is lost.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Strategic Speed Upgrades">
    Regularly invest in Run Speed upgrades. As your Kick Power increases, so should your ability to outrun the tsunami. A balanced approach ensures you can capitalize on every high-value kick.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Plot Security">
    Once safely back, place the Brainrot on your plot without delay. It only contributes to your income once secured.
  </Step>
</Steps>
