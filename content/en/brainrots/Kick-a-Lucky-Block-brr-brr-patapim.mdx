<Section icon={Shield} title="Tsunami Survival: Securing Your Rewards">
The tsunami is a critical challenge after every kick in *Kick a Lucky Block*. If the wave catches you before you return to your plot with a Brainrot, that hard-earned reward is lost. Effective tsunami survival requires balancing Kick Power with Run Speed.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Kick the Lucky Block">
    Start each run by kicking the Lucky Block from your plot or safe kicking area. The landing distance determines the zone and Brainrot quality.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Watch the Landing Distance">
    Use the zone reached by the block to judge how difficult the return run will be. Farther zones mean longer, riskier chases.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Become the Brainrot and Turn Back Fast">
    Once the Brainrot appears, immediately move back toward your plot or safe zone. Delay is costly as the tsunami starts chasing quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Avoid the Tsunami Wave">
    Keep moving directly toward safety and avoid unnecessary detours. If the wave catches you, the Brainrot is lost.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Place the Brainrot on Your Plot">
    After reaching safety, place the Brainrot to start generating passive cash. Unclaimed high-value rolls do not help progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Upgrade Run Speed">
    Spend cash on Speed upgrades near the Weight Shop as you start reaching farther zones. Matching Speed with Kick Power is vital.
  </Step>
  <Step num="7" title="Balance Kick Power and Speed">
    Do not chase Secret, Divine, Hacked, OG, or Celestial zones solely through raw Kick Power. Losing rolls to the tsunami is worse than safely farming a slightly lower zone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="8" title="Use Rebirth and Mutations">
    Build permanent cash multipliers through Rebirth and prioritize mutated high-tier Brainrots you can safely return with. More income means faster Speed and weight upgrades.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
Never underestimate the tsunami. Losing a high-value Brainrot due to insufficient Run Speed can significantly hinder your progress.
</Callout>
