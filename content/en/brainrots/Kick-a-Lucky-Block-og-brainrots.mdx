<Section icon={Trophy} title="Optimizing Your Progression for OG Brainrots">
Achieving consistent OG Brainrot farming requires a well-planned progression path. This involves strategic upgrades, timely rebirths, and effective plot management.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Maximize Kick Power">
    Continuously purchase the best weights available to increase your Kick Power. The "Giant Gold Star Barbell" is the most expensive listed weight, offering +100,000 Kick Power and is a key target for reaching the OG zone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Run Speed">
    As your Kick Power grows, invest heavily in Run Speed upgrades. Kicking into the OG zone means a very long return trip, and without sufficient speed, your valuable Brainrots will be lost to the tsunami. Balance Kick Power and Run Speed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Strategic Rebirths">
    Rebirths provide permanent cash multipliers, making it faster to afford high-cost upgrades like endgame weights. Before rebirthing, ensure your plot is filled with the highest-value Brainrots you can obtain, as the multiplier will apply to their earnings.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Plot Management">
    Regularly replace lower-value Brainrots on your plot with stronger, mutated ones, especially as you start pulling from the OG zone. A full plot of high-earning Brainrots is essential for rapid cash generation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Leverage Luck Events">
    Keep an eye out for Global Luck events or admin abuse periods. These events significantly boost your chances of rolling rare Brainrots and powerful mutations, making them ideal times to push for OG Brainrots.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Rebirth Timing">
The first rebirth is available at 1,000 Kick Power and gives a 2x cash multiplier. For stronger progression, it's often better to push one or two zones deeper, collect better Brainrots, then rebirth so the multiplier applies to a stronger earning base.
</Callout>
